# ETG_summer-olympics-data-analysis
Through the given data of summer olympics , I have tried to analyze the Number of cities where olympics are held so far.
I have also visualize the sports with most number of medals and also most number of  gold medals so far.
Also tried to visualize the players with most number of medals and gold medals.
I have also visualize and listed the events popular in terms of the number of players to know the interest of players.
By comparing with countries I have tried to find out the year when India won first gold medal.
